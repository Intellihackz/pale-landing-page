Features

RGB and HEX Support

How It Works

Click the Pale icon to extract colors from the current webpage. Our advanced algorithm detects the most prominent colors.

View the extracted colors in an organized grid. Each color is displayed with its RGB and HEX values.

Easily copy the RGB or HEX values of any color with a single click. Perfect for use in your designs or code.

How to Install

Click the "Download" button at the top of this page to get the Pale extension ZIP file.
Locate the downloaded ZIP file and extract its contents to a folder on your computer.
In Chrome, navigate to chrome://extensions or click Menu > More Tools > Extensions.
Toggle on the "Developer mode" switch in the top right corner of the Extensions page.
Click "Load unpacked" and select the folder where you extracted the Pale extension files.
